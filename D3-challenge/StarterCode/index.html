Correlations between being a smoker and age

From the scatterplot created from the data given. The average age of smokers throughout the United States ranges from age 30-44. The percentage of smokers per state ranges from 10% up to 27%. It would be interesting to compare the national average to all the states. The average age is in the middle of a typical lifespan. It would be interesting to see how many folks give up smoking once they reach 50+. Considering health issues caused by smoking.
